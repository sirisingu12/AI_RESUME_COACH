Data Analyst — Business Intelligence

About the role:
We are seeking a detail-oriented Data Analyst to join our Business
Intelligence team. You will transform raw data into actionable
insights, build dashboards, and work with stakeholders across the
business to drive data-informed decision-making.

Responsibilities:
- Collect, clean, and analyse large datasets from multiple sources.
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports using BI tools.
- Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities in data.
- Present findings clearly to both technical and non-technical
  stakeholders.
- Collaborate with engineering teams to improve data pipelines and
  data quality.

Requirements:
- Strong proficiency in SQL (complex queries, window functions, CTEs).
- Experience with Python for data analysis (pandas, numpy, matplotlib).
- Familiarity with BI tools: Tableau, Power BI, or Looker.
- Solid understanding of statistics and data visualisation principles.
- Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

Nice to have:
- Experience with cloud data warehouses (BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift).
- Knowledge of dbt for data transformation.
- Exposure to machine learning concepts.
- Experience with A/B testing and experimentation frameworks.

What we offer:
- Collaborative and data-driven culture.
- Mentorship from senior data professionals.
- Clear career growth path into data science or data engineering.
